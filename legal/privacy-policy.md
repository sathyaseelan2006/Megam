# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 5, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Megam ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage when you use our air quality monitoring platform at [https://megam-n2z95mbl0-sathyaseelan2006s-projects.vercel.app](https://megam-n2z95mbl0-sathyaseelan2006s-projects.vercel.app).

By using Megam, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Location Data**: When you search for a city or click on the globe, we temporarily process geographic coordinates (latitude/longitude) to fetch air quality data. We do not store your location data on our servers.
- **Search History**: If you use our history feature, search queries are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Browser Information**: Browser type, version, operating system, and device information.
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, time spent on the site, and interactions with the 3D globe.
- **IP Address**: Temporarily logged for security and analytics purposes (anonymized where possible).
- **Cookies**: We use cookies to improve user experience and analytics (see Section 3).

### 1.3 Third-Party Data Sources
We fetch real-time air quality and weather data from the following external services:
- **IQAir AirVisual API** - Air quality data from 80,000+ monitoring stations worldwide
- **OpenAQ API** - Government monitoring station data from 100+ countries
- **WAQI (World Air Quality Index)** - Aggregated air quality data
- **NASA POWER API** - Satellite aerosol optical depth measurements

**Important**: We do not control these third-party services. Please review their privacy policies:
- IQAir: [https://www.iqair.com/privacy-policy](https://www.iqair.com/privacy-policy)
- OpenAQ: [https://openaq.org/privacy](https://openaq.org/privacy)
- NASA: [https://www.nasa.gov/about/highlights/HP_Privacy.html](https://www.nasa.gov/about/highlights/HP_Privacy.html)

## 3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### 3.1 What Cookies We Use
- **Essential Cookies**: Required for basic site functionality (search history, preferences)
- **Analytics Cookies**: Track usage patterns and site performance (Google Analytics or similar)
- **Performance Cookies**: Monitor site speed and optimize loading times

### 3.2 Cookie Consent
When you first visit Megam, we display a cookie consent banner. You can:
- **Accept**: Allow all cookies for full functionality
- **Decline**: Use essential cookies only (some features may be limited)

You can change your cookie preferences anytime by clearing browser data or adjusting browser settings.

### 3.3 How to Disable Cookies
Most browsers allow you to control cookies through settings:
- **Chrome**: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
- **Firefox**: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
- **Safari**: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
- **Edge**: Settings > Privacy > Cookies

**Note**: Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where Data is Stored
- **Browser Storage**: Search history, favorites, and ML model weights are stored locally in your browser (localStorage and IndexedDB). This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it.
- **Server Logs**: Temporary server logs (IP addresses, request timestamps) are stored for security and debugging purposes. These are automatically deleted after 30 days.
- **No User Accounts**: Megam does not require user accounts or store personal information on our servers.

### 4.2 Security Measures
- **HTTPS Encryption**: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS.
- **API Key Protection**: Third-party API keys are stored securely on our servers and never exposed to client-side code.
- **Rate Limiting**: We implement rate limiting to prevent abuse and protect our infrastructure.

### 4.3 Data Retention
- **Browser Data**: Retained locally until you clear browser storage
- **Server Logs**: Automatically deleted after 30 days
- **Analytics**: Aggregated usage data retained for 12 months
